## What was built

**Phase 1: Conversion sprint (2 weeks, Nov 2025)**
- 3 product landing pages redesigned (Body Safety, Puberty, Mom Rage)
- Order bumps and thank-you page upsells across 4 core products
- 4 ManyChat flows with age-based tagging and handoff
- Post-purchase and cart abandonment email sequences
- Body Safety Workbook live sale (end-to-end)

**Phase 2: Launches and infrastructure (Dec 2025 – Apr 2026)**
- 8 launches run or supported
- Shop page rebuilt as product library
- Nurturing Faith repositioned from $49 to $99 with full sales page
- Coaching page rebuilt for discovery call conversion
- Backend cleanup (deactivated dozens of legacy offers)
- Senja testimonial system rebuilt

## Key lessons

1. Reply automation beats link stickers (reach went 13K → 33K)
2. Order bumps add 15-25% revenue with zero extra traffic
3. Audience was price-sensitive, not disinterested
4. Friday outperformed Saturday for sales
