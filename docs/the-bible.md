# The Bible — Outreach Methodology

Single source of truth for the outreach playbook.

## Core context

Freelance technical partner for parenting & faith-based coaches (Kajabi + GHL funnels/pages/sequences). NOT an automation freelancer. The business problem is making money. Bottleneck is conversion (not lead supply). The leak is **turn-two** — openers work, turn-two is where threads die. Zero cold closes. IG account banned Jun 22, 2026. Cold email is primary channel.

## Working rules (learned the hard way)

1. **Verbatim only.** Exact text sent, never cleaned-up versions.
2. **Never fabricate.** Audit findings and logs are verified/confirmed.
3. **One message = one row** in outreach logs.
4. **Read-signals on every logged lead** — what YES means (deliver FAST) and what silence means (park/warm-nurture).
5. **Push back on me.** I value disagreement over agreement.
6. **Writing style:** no em-dashes, no AI rhythm, no parallel three-beat, no consultant-speak.
7. **Notion formatting:** real paragraph breaks. Observations in page BODY, not properties.
8. **Re-fetch Notion page before editing** — needs exact character matching.

## The core finding: funnel breaks at turn-two

Not the opener. Not the channel. Not targeting. One message: the one sent right after the lead shows interest. The opener earns the reply; turn-two gives it back. The fix: at turn-two, make ONE concrete artifact offer or ONE single-tap next step. Never a soft exit, menu, or re-pitch.

## Winning messages pattern

Every winning opener:
- Leads with a specific, verifiable leak the person can check in 30 seconds
- Reads as genuine human who actually looked, not a template
- Ends on ONE real question or ONE concrete offer
- NOT praise-led

## The rules

**Rule 1 — Channel:** Email is primary. IG for sourcing only.

**Rule 2 — The opener:** Specific verifiable leak, not praise. One line of warmth max. End on ONE real question. Verify leak from clean state (incognito, logged out).

**Rule 2b — "Nothing happened" ≠ broken.** Check if delivery is automated or manual before asserting a broken freebie.

**Rule 2c — Best fit, no opener → warm-up.** Don't force a leak.

**Rule 3 — Turn-two is the whole game.** ONE concrete artifact offer or one single-tap next step.

**Rule 4 — Kill the soft exit.** "No pressure/no rush/if it's useful" — hunt it in every message.

**Rule 5 — Match artifact to decision-making.** Solo → Loom/mockup. Team → forwardable Loom.

**Rule 6 — Single-tap ask.** Calendly link, not "tomorrow morning?"

**Rule 7 — Deliver artifacts FAST.** Yes → within the hour (warm) or same day (max).

**Rule 8 — Two-touch limit is IG-only.** Email follows Email OS cadence. Don't bury leads early.

**Rule 9 — Channel continuation, not switch.** Continuing warm on the channel they engaged is clean. Switching reads as chasing.

## The standard motion

1. Find a creator with real audience + verifiable funnel leak.
2. Verify the leak from clean state. Never fabricate.
3. Open via cold email — subject + specific finding + one question.
4. They reply. Track every send.
5. Turn-two: ONE concrete artifact offer or single-tap step.
6. Yes → deliver artifact FAST. Loom on their live page, same day.
7. Book call with Calendly link.
8. Call → proposal → close.

## Sourcing & qualifying pipeline

**Gate 0 — Where leads come from:** Mine follow-lists of in-niche anchor accounts. Big anchor → mine FOLLOWERS. Mid-size peer → mine FOLLOWING.

**Step 0.5 — The floor (added Jul 2, 2026, from pipeline evidence):** A different check from Gate 0 above — Gate 0 is about sourcing, the floor is a minimum enforced at walk time because the rows prove sourcing doesn't always catch it. Audience floor: roughly 1K followers or an equivalent real audience signal (podcast, list, active community). Receipt: Blanka Kellermayer, 115 followers, three touches burned on an account that can't pay even the smallest price point. Activity floor: last post or visible activity within ~3 weeks, checked against a live search, not secondhand notes. Receipt: Lisa Chan, 7 weeks silent, correctly parked, but only by instinct — now it's a rule. Niche floor: parenting or faith-based, genuinely; adjacent wellness niches without the case-study fit get parked. Receipt: Gayu Lewis, menopause coach, her own row said "not parenting" and got three touches anyway. Failing the floor = Lane 3, reason noted, stop — touches are the scarcest resource in a crisis.

**Gate 1 — Gatekeeper check:** Team/gatekeeper between you and owner? YES → Skip. NO → Proceed.

**The funnel walk (5 stops):**
1. Bio link — one clear next step or pile of choices?
2. Freebie — can a stranger get something free + does it ask for email?
3. Offer/sales page — in 10 seconds, can I tell what it is, the price, why I want it?
4. Checkout — if I tried to pay, would anything stop me?
5. Audience ownership — if IG vanished, could they reach these people?

**Tier system (per flagged finding):**
- Tier A — Critical. Blocks conversion entirely. Provably broken. Opener-grade on its own.
- Tier B — Strong. Real money leaking. Needs some framing but the cost is real and felt.
- Tier C — Soft. Technically suboptimal, low felt cost. Rarely a Lane 1 opener alone; needs the sting test to survive.

**Master principle:** A leak is any point where attention the coach already earned fails to convert toward money she can see — AND she'd feel the cost if you named it.

**Two filters:**
- Sting test: would she FEEL this, or shrug?
- Vitamin filter: FELT COST or a MECHANISM she lacks? Mechanisms are vitamins.

**Three lanes:**
- Lane 1 — OPEN: felt leak + committed buyer. Conversion lane.
- Lane 2 — WARM-UP: committed buyer, no felt leak. Intel lane.
- Lane 3 — KILL: not a buyer. Park.

**Coherence rule (added Jul 2, 2026):** Lane 3 forces Tier 4 + Status Disqualified. Lane 1 or 2 can never carry Tier 4.

**The opening angle, required companions (added Jul 2, 2026):**
- Innocent explanation: Lane 1 findings must carry a second line — the plausible non-blame reason the leak exists (calendar might just need a reset, date might be stuck, section might still be loading). Feeds the either/or closing question in the email. No plausible explanation → flag it; the finding may read as an accusation no matter how it's phrased.
- Hook type label: when an SMYKM hook exists, label it WORK (her framework, content, testimonial, point of view), LIFE (birthday, personal post), or METRIC (numbers she owns).

**Vision-pass gate (added Jul 2, 2026):** `python main.py vision check evidence/<slug>` must print `VISION PASS: COMPLETE` before a walk's findings are used. Machine flags from the crawler are candidates only — a flag that fails visual confirmation on the screenshot is dead and stays dead.

## The artifact engine

**Tier 1 — Loom:** 3-5 min screen-recorded walkthrough of THEIR live page. Face + voice = trust. Structure: 0:00-0:30 confirm leak → 0:30-2:30 show fix → 2:30-3:00 what it looks like.

**Tier 2 — Mockup page:** Only when warm. Never lead with this cold.

## The Loom script

Beat 1 — Warm open (~15s). Beat 2 — Show leak LIVE (~30-45s). Beat 3 — Show fix (~2 min). Beat 4 — The ask (~20s). One concrete next step. "Either way the audit's yours to keep" makes the no painless without removing the yes.
