## The logic

Value = (dream outcome x likelihood) / (time delay x effort). The winnable half for a solo operator is the bottom: make it instant and effortless. 48-hour delivery with one login and zero meetings is the moat.

Risk is the whole objection. Lead with risk reversal, not deliverables.
